# Krita.ai Acquisition

**Metrics:** $1.2M acquisition led | 18-month roadmap acceleration | 3 products in portfolio

## Framing

A capital-constrained build vs buy decision: no existing agentic AI capability, an 18-month internal build estimate, and a competitive window closing. $1.2M was deployed to collapse the roadmap gap through acquisition  -  requiring full ownership of strategy and execution, from technical due diligence to board alignment. This is the kind of resource allocation judgment a founder-CEO needs to see: when to acquire, how much to deploy, and what it buys.

## Context

Enterprise clients were requesting agentic AI capabilities. Internal build estimates put the timeline at 18+ months. Waiting was a competitive risk. Peers were shipping agentic features faster.

## Decision

Led the $1.2M M&A evaluation end-to-end: technical due diligence, product-market fit assessment against our client base, integration roadmap design, and stakeholder alignment.

## Action

Identified Krita.ai as the best fit for accelerating the agentic AI layer without disrupting the core platform.

## Outcome

Roadmap accelerated by 18 months. A new revenue stream was added to the portfolio. The acquisition strengthened our agentic AI positioning in GCC enterprise sales conversations, moving from a roadmap promise to a shipped capability.
