# Rebuilding the Enterprise Win Rate

**Metrics:** 14→35% RFP win rate | 27 scoring dimensions | $25M+ pipeline managed

## Context

Sales was pursuing every RFP regardless of fit. Win rate plateaued at 14%. There was no framework to distinguish winnable opportunities from noise, and RFP responses were written in product language rather than buyer language.

## Decision

Built a 27-dimension opportunity scoring model that segmented prospects into four CX maturity tiers. Repositioned RFP responses around buyer language, matching the client's stated pain to our differentiated capability. Introduced a qualification gate before committing presales resources.

## Action

This work was happening while the platform was scaling from zero to $6M ARR, so each percentage point of win rate carried direct revenue consequence.

## Outcome

Win rate climbed from 14% to 35%. The qualification framework became standard presales practice across the team. Pipeline quality improved, reducing wasted cycles on low-fit deals.
