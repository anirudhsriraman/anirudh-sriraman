# CAPE Framework: CX Analytics & Product Execution

A collection of operational frameworks used across product, analytics, and team leadership at Dropthought and Bahwan CyberTek.

---

## DEAR Model: Churn Prediction

Predicts churn 6 months before renewal using four dimensions:

- **D**: Deployment (platform configured and live)
- **E**: Engagement (users active and returning)
- **A**: Adoption (workflows embedded in daily work)
- **R**: ROI (client can articulate measurable value)

Clients with healthy Deployment scores had **15% higher gross renewal rates**.

---

## CS Analyst Framework: X/O Model

Six analysis patterns combining experience (X) and operational (O) data:

| Pattern | Use |
|---------|-----|
| **X Why** | Find operational explanation for an experience data signal |
| **O Drivers** | Find operational situation causing the experience data |
| **X&O Predict** | Build projections from combined data |
| **X&O Personalise** | Adjust treatment based on combined signals |
| **X&O Alert** | Send proactive alerts on combined signals |
| **X Value** | Measure experience improvement impact on business results |

## BAU North Star

> "Best Active User identifies and shares insight consumed by 5+ people in 30 days, leading to 3+ documented improvements in a quarter."

Used to set product engagement targets and design activation flows.

---

## Three-Layer Metrics Architecture

**Signal quality → Experience quality → Business outcomes**

Built Tableau connecting all three layers into a single leadership view.

> "CX programs lose organisational credibility when metrics are reported in isolation."

---

## Engagement Funnel (Mixpanel)

Four-stage funnel: first-time → new → ordinary → **engaged**

**Engaged user definition:** logs in every weekday, spends 30+ minutes weekly, responds to 5%+ of feedback items.

---

## Client Health Scoring (Totango)

Eight-dimension health score with weights derived from renewal correlations.

Key finding: users with **high NPS but declining engagement** predicted silent churn.

Retention improved from **70% to 96%** after health score integration.

---

## PM Career Ladder

Five-dimension ladder from Associate PM through Principal PM:

1. Insight
2. Strategy
3. Execution
4. Outcomes
5. Leadership

**Ownership model by engineering effort:**
- S and M features: full PM authority
- L, XL, XXL features: written brief required

> "This gave junior PMs real authority from day one."

---

## Change Frameworks

### Waterfall → Agile
Two-track sprint planning, staggered engineering cycles, structured backlog governance.
Result: **50% improvement in development velocity**.

### Design Thinking
Restructured team into journey-aligned pods. Quarterly persona cadence. Shifted culture from belief-based to evidence-based decisions.

### AI Adoption
Automated user research analysis, FAQ updates, release notes. AI-assisted QA recovered three weeks of timeline.

### CX Accountability Culture
Universal NPS distribution. Decoupled response rate from CS metrics. Embedded retention, NPS, ease-of-use as shared OKRs.
